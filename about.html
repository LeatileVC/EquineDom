Our History

The Beginning

Founded in 1964 by equine enthusiast Kagiso Tselaesele, EquineDom began as a small breeding operation with just three mares and one stallion, a Boere Perd named Battallion.Kagiso's vision was to create a breeding facility that prioritized both the health of the horses and the preservation of exceptional bloodlines.

What started in the dusty farm in Kenna Transcended into one of the most recognised equine breeding operations in Botswana.

Our Philosophy

At EquineDom, we believe that exceptional horses come from a combination of superior genetics, meticulous care, and thoughtful training. We don't just breed horses - we bring forth champion genetics in every stable.

Every decision we make, from selecting breeding pairs to designing individual nutrition plans, is guided by our commitment to excellence and our deep respect for these magnificent animals.

Why We Do It

"Horses are not just our business - they're our passion, our pride. Every foal born at EquineDom represents generations of careful breeding and the potential for greatness. That's what drives us every day."

- Kagiso Tselaesele, Founder
